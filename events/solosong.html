Event Rules

  • Participants will get a time duration of 3-4 minutes for their performance.
  • There will be negative marking if the participant does not adhere to the time limit.
  • Performance on classical/semi-classical songs is allowed in the eastern category.
  • No English songs are allowed for the performance.
  • Contestants can use their own downloaded karaoke (backing track) or bring their own instruments if required in the performance.
  • The song lyrics must not contain any profanity or political allusions inappropriate for a family-oriented environment.
  • The song and lyrics must be memorized.
  • No video screens will be allowed to be used during the performance.
  • Attractive gift hampers & certificates will be given to final top 3 winner
  • E-certificates will be given to all the participants.
